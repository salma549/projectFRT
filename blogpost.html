Blog Post: COVID-19

As the world continues to grapple with the COVID-19 pandemic, it's important to stay informed and take steps to protect ourselves and others. Here are some key points to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • Avoid close contact with people who are sick.
  • Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue or your elbow when you cough or sneeze.
  • Wear a mask when you're in public or around others who aren't in your household.
  • Stay home if you're feeling sick or have been exposed to someone with COVID-19.

Remember, we're all in this together. Let's do our part to keep ourselves and our communities safe.
